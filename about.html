Goodbye Warden is a project by an anonymous creator that went offline in 2021. It is resurrected here by Morry Kolman (WTTDOTM). The original project can still be found on the Wayback Machine.

The death penalty is a moral stain on our society. Since 1973, at least 202 people who were wrongly convicted and sentenced to death in the U.S. have been exonerated.
